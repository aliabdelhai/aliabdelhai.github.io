BioSense ECG100

About the Product

The BioSense ECG100 is our top-of-the-line 12-lead interpretive electrocardiograph. This advanced ECG system provides fast, accurate analysis of 12-lead ECG waveforms and rhythms using the latest algorithm technology. The ECG100 produces clinical quality 12-lead ECGs with high fidelity digital signal acquisition. The built-in interpretive algorithm provides rapid and accurate evaluation of ECG findings for improved clinical diagnosis.

Key Features

  • 12-lead simultaneous ECG acquisition
  • High resolution color display
  • Touchscreen interface
  • Interpretive analysis of arrhythmias
  • Wireless networking and EMR connectivity
  • Adult and pediatric ECG capabilities
  • Fast charge battery for portability
